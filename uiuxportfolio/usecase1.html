Coinside Betting

Designing a web application for Coinside Betting

Project Summary

Coinside Betting is a betting company in Netherlands, it wishes to come up with a betting platform that supports crypcurrencies and NFT.The target of the company is to develop a platform for the "Gen-Z" & "the millenials" aged 18 years to 45 years living in South America.

Challenge

I am tasked to research on the existing market and identify the users needs and design & interaction challanges faced by the users on other gaming platforms

I am tasked to design a cross-platform application that will be loved by our target market

Research

User Feedback poll : from users reviews, comments and ratings I realized complex navigation patterns ,poor website layout & too much irrelevant information on the website was a pain to most users

Competitor Analysis : I have Benchmarked popular gambling websites and noticed a simple navigation pattern is used, a responsive design wiith upto 5 breakpoints is used and only relevant content is displayed.
