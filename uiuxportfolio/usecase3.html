Space ya Tech

Designing a Tech Community Forum

Project Summary

Space ya Tech is a Kenyan community forum where tech entusiasts get to meet, network & share with one another

Challenge

This being a community project.The platform is to be designed by the community.As part of the community project I am requested to collaboratively work on:-

The style guides,Wireframes & Low fidelity Designs of the platform

the designs are expected to comfortably suit the users needs when interacting with the platform

Research

User Feedback poll : from users reviews, comments and ratings on other blogging forums I realized complex navigation patterns, some had poor website layout & some had too much irrelevant information on the website which was a pain to most users who simply wanted to network ,communicate and get help from their peers

Competitor Analysis : I have Benchmarked popular community forums such as Stack overflow and noticed a simple navigation pattern is used, a responsive design wiith upto 5 breakpoints is used and only relevant content is displayed to its users.
